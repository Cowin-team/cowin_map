About Us

India is going through a crisis with the second wave of Covid19. Some social media posts asked "How can you help if you are living away from India", others asked "Where can you donate money". Meanwhile, two overwhelmed young Indians, Nivedita Mahesh and Aishwarya struggled to find resources for their family members or friends affected with Covid in India. It was difficult to do so without added issues such as driving through traffic or going far without knowing if the resource was still available. They realized that there needed to be a way to have a central dataset on a location based UI. Within a few days, along with two other friends, they gitbrought cowinmap.com to life.

That was in April 2021. Since then, The Cowin Map has become 45+ volunteers strong, working in shifts across the US, India, Singapore, Germany, updating resources every few hours, making calls, verifying resources. The strength of a community during a crisis is powerful and unmatched.

This is new. This is exhilarating.

Cowinmap started in the midst of the second wave in India. In less than a month (May 31st) Cowinmap is bolstered by a group of 70 (students & professionals) from across the world, has about 6000 unique vistiors of the website per week, 800 instagram followers, and serves 12 states and 240 districts in India.

We map out the following resources:

  • Hospital beds
  • Oxygen suppliers
  • Covid meals
  • Triage centers/Covid war rooms

The hospital information is refreshed every 3 hours. The Oxygen suppliers are called and verified by our volunteers everyday.
